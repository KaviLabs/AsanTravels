About Asanka

Hi, I’m Asanka Rajapakse – a passionate and experienced tour guide with over 10 years in the travel industry. With more than 700 successful tours led, I’ve had the pleasure of helping travelers from around the world explore the beauty, history, and culture of our destinations.

I’m fluent in English, which helps me connect easily with international visitors and ensure they feel comfortable and well-informed throughout their journey. Whether it’s your first visit or your tenth, I aim to make every tour personal, memorable, and truly special.

Why visit Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a land of breathtaking beauty and endless adventure. From golden beaches and lush rainforests to ancient cities and vibrant festivals, this island paradise offers something for every traveler. Discover UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Sigiriya and Kandy, go on thrilling safaris to see elephants and leopards, or simply relax with a cup of world-famous Ceylon tea.

  • Stunning beaches and scenic landscapes
  • Ancient ruins and cultural heritage
  • Wildlife safaris and adventure sports
  • Delicious local cuisine
  • Warm and welcoming people
  • Colorful festivals year-round
